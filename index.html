About Us

Discover our restaurant story

From very humble beginnings the group which established the Punjab Sweet House, at the dawn of the Indian Independence, took the next logical step of opening a restaurant named Kaveri in Bilaspur. Kaveri in no time became the most popular restaurant in the city providing affordable quality. Kaveri’s Catering Division was the next step to cater to offsite large functions and events.
